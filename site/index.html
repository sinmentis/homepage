About

Engineering across the full stack of systems

I started close to the metal — bare-metal firmware and embedded Linux for connected appliances and marine displays — and worked my way up through the Linux kernel, Android AOSP, and large-scale build infrastructure. Along the way I kept gravitating toward the things that make teams faster: pipelines, tooling, and reliability.

These days I work on cloud-native development and developer productivity — designing CI/CD that people trust, hardening distributed systems, and removing the friction between an idea and a shipped, observable change.

Cloud-Native & CI/CD

Pipelines and automation people trust — GitHub Actions, containerized multi-platform builds, zero-downtime deploys.

Platforms & Embedded

From the Linux kernel and Android AOSP down to embedded firmware in C and C++ — debugging bootloaders to user space.

Developer Productivity

Tooling, static analysis, and build systems that cut feedback loops and let engineers ship with confidence.
